Our Services

Here at BTG we provide retirement homes with a variety of staffing options to support absenteeism, vacations, short and long term needs. We are excited to partner with your Human Resources department to fill any gaps that are currently available. Our mandate is to solely focus on you and your individual needs. We look forward to working alongside you and providing exceptional service.  Please see below the services that we provide:

Registered Nurses (RN & RPN)

For optimal health, our Registered Nurses and Licensed/Registered Practical Nurses help prevent illness and increase comfort at home.

Personal Support Workers (PSW)

Our PSW’s provide various personal care services such as: bathing, dressing, feeding, walking and incontinence support.


Servers

Our Food Servers will interact with senior residents and guests, take and deliver food orders in a timely manner following company standards, and ensure everyone is happy with their meal.

Kitchen Porters

Our Kitchen porters will maintain the cleanliness and orderliness

Dietary
Aide

Our certified Dietary aiders are trained to offer residents nutritious meals whilst following allergen guidelines.

Light House Keeping

We offer light housekeeping services to prevent cross contamination
